Via Gustavo is a walking simulator game in Google Street View.

Built by Jakub Fiala during the HeyHuman residency in 2024.

Finding Gustavo

Set out on a walk with a long, lonely road. Enjoy the view — take it as slow as you like.

  • Use headphones for the best sound experience
  • Designed for desktop + tablet devices
